Terms And Condition

1. Scope of Services

TreeConnect Sàrl is a Swiss-based consulting firm offering customized services to support the operational and strategic needs of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Our expertise covers a broad range of business functions, including:
- Business planning and launch support
- Human resources and people operations
- Administrative and back-office organization
- IT and infrastructure coordination
- Accounting and financial process improvement
- Marketing and communication advisory

Services may include strategic consulting, operational setup, process design, project support, and externalized solutions tailored to each client's goals and stage of growth.

2. Engagement Model

All services provided by TreeConnect are subject to a specific agreement established between TreeConnect and the client. This contract defines the scope of work, timelines, responsibilities, and applicable fees.
These general terms serve as a base reference and are complemented or superseded by the terms of the signed agreement.

3. Availability and Communication

TreeConnect is available to its clients Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 18:00 (CET), excluding Swiss public holidays. The communication methods (e.g., email, phone, secure tools) are defined in the client-specific agreement. We aim to respond to all professional inquiries within one business day.

All data and information shared by the client is treated as strictly confidential and will not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent, unless required by law.
